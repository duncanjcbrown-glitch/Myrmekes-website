How engagements work

Quotations are issued subject to Cantel's standard terms. The mechanics, in plain English:

  • Quote validity: 90 days for devices in mainland UK; 30 days outside.
  • Getting started: contract creation begins on receipt of a valid purchase order.
  • Ramp-up: new maintenance contracts carry a 14-day ramp-up while sparing is put in place and asset data is verified — faults in that window are handled on a reasonable-endeavours basis, with the full SLA applying after.
  • What we need from you: full device specifications and site addresses — they're what let the SLA be enforced and the pricing hold.
  • Standard exclusions: consumables, solid-state drives, disk retention, firmware, software, externally connected devices, tape drives, and UPS units — unless stated in the quotation. RAID cache batteries can be included on larger contracts by request.
  • Pre-existing faults: issues that predate the contract are chargeable on a time-and-materials basis unless otherwise agreed.
