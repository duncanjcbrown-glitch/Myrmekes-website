What to do with a lifecycle date

Validate the asset

Confirm the full model, serial or service tag, configuration, drive and enclosure inventory, site and current cover.

Separate hardware and software

Independent hardware support does not automatically include Dell software, OE, firmware, licensing or engineering entitlement.

Choose the route

Compare OEM renewal, assessed independent support, a hybrid arrangement or refresh against business risk and the migration plan.

Need help interpreting an estate?

Send the asset list and locations. We will identify what can be assessed for UK hardware cover and what should remain with Dell.
