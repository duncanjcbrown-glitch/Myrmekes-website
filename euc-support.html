EUC Device Support

Damage and breakdown cover across your mobile devices, tablets, and laptops — one annual fee that covers the whole workforce. No per-repair invoicing, no procurement cycle every time a screen cracks.

How it works

  • 1. Raise a ticket — log the fault when a device breaks.
  • 2. Fault diagnosed — the device is assessed and the repair agreed.
  • 3. Back within 72 hours — repaired and returned, with little impact on the user.

What's included

  • Faulty buttons, ports, and audio issues
  • Cameras and lenses
  • Batteries and housing
  • Liquid damage
  • Extended warranty cover

Optional extras

Remote monitoring of hardware status and performance — spot failing devices before users do.

Who runs on this today

Through the Cantel network: a global financial services firm with 2,200 devices supported (Microsoft Surface Pro and Apple iPad), a European retailer with 1,300 devices including Android phones and headsets for home workers, and a UK insurance company with 2,600 tablets. More delivery examples →

Why one annual fee

  • One easy payment — no ongoing invoicing or admin per repair
  • No wait times negotiating each fix
  • Low impact on user productivity

Cover your fleet

Tell us your device count and mix, and we'll shape the plan. If we can't help, we'll say so quickly.
