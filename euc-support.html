EUC Device Support

Damage and breakdown cover across your mobile devices, tablets, and laptops — one annual fee that covers the whole workforce. No per-repair invoicing, no procurement cycle every time a screen cracks.

How it works

  • 1. Raise a ticket — log the fault when a device breaks.
  • 2. Fault diagnosed — the device is assessed and the repair agreed.
  • 3. Repair, return, or replace — completed according to the service tier agreed for your estate.

What's included

  • Faulty buttons, ports, and audio issues
  • Cameras and lenses
  • Batteries and housing, where expressly included in the plan
  • Liquid or accidental damage, where included in the plan
  • Extended warranty cover

Optional extras

Remote monitoring of hardware status and performance — spot failing devices before users do.

Examples from Cantel's EUC portfolio

The documented estates include 2,200 Microsoft Surface Pro devices for a global financial-services organisation, 1,300 Apple iPads for a European retailer, and 2,600 tablets for a UK insurance company, alongside an Android home-user estate including phones and headsets.
