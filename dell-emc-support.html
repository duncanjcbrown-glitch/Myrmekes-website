How Dell EMC support is delivered

  1. Identify the estate. Share model numbers, service tags or serials, quantities, configuration and site addresses.
  2. Define the outcome. Tell us whether you need break/fix cover, a bridge to refresh, an estate move or another professional service.
  3. Assess risk and coverage. Cantel checks engineering, parts, travel, access, support hours and the requested response.
  4. Confirm the contract. The quotation and statement of work state supported assets, service levels, inclusions, exclusions and commercial terms.

UK and international delivery

Cantel delivers support and professional services in the UK through 66 field engineers across six regional depots: Newark-upon-Trent, Leeds, Croydon, Glasgow, Belfast and Bristol. Outside the UK, delivery is provided through Cantel-approved service partners and affiliates. Exact country, vendor, response, parts and travel coverage is confirmed for each engagement.

What is not assumed

No blanket promise is made for every Dell EMC model or every location. Response times depend on the equipment, site, parts plan, access, support window and agreed contract. Dell software, firmware, licensing, configuration changes, backup, restore and data recovery are not included unless explicitly stated.

What determines the price

Pricing is built around the actual risk and delivery requirement rather than a generic online rate. The main inputs are the model and configuration, equipment quantity and age, site location, requested support hours and response, parts strategy, access requirements, contract length and any project work. A clean asset list produces the fastest, most reliable quotation.

Dell EMC support questions

Does Myrmekes support Dell EMC storage after warranty?

Yes, subject to an estate assessment. VNX and Unity are specifically covered by this content cluster; other Dell EMC storage families can be assessed individually.

Can you guarantee a response time?

We confirm the available response and support window after checking the exact equipment and delivery location. The contractual commitment appears in the quotation or statement of work.

Are parts new?

The agreed parts strategy may use new, equivalent or refurbished components. The contract defines what applies to the covered estate.

Does this include Dell software and firmware?

No, not automatically. Hardware is the core proposition. Software, firmware, licences, configuration and data services must be expressly included.
