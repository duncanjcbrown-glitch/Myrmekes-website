About

I'm Duncan Brown, and I've spent 20 years selling and delivering IT infrastructure and services into global enterprises.

My background

I started in value-add distribution, moved into infrastructure and security managed services, then spent time building and leading services businesses of my own — before a run in enterprise SaaS sales with AirWatch by VMware, and later building channel businesses across storage and cybersecurity-focused services. Along the way I've sold into and delivered for organisations including Nokia, Honda, KPMG, Rightmove, Lockheed Martin, and Imperial Tobacco Group.

The number I'm proudest of: in one year at ControlCircle, I delivered £1.19M of gross margin against a £400K target — 300% of what was asked.

I also co-authored Cantel's guide to choosing the right Dell EMC support partner, drawing on that same hands-on experience of what actually matters when an estate needs support.

The thinking behind the name

Why Myrmekes

Myrmekes exists to put that experience directly behind end users — engineering resources, support contracts, and fulfilment, delivered through the same trusted networks I've built relationships with over two decades: Cantel's engineers, and the Cloud CoCo relationship.
